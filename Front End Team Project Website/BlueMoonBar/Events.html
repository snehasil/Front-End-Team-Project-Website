Craft Beer Pinball League

Join us every 1st and 3rd Wednesday for some Beer and Pinball. Meet brewery reps, have a beer, and play some pinball in a relaxed setting. Everyone from all skill levels is welcome! Every other week enjoy featured taps and other surprises as we play some silver ball. Group play starts around 6:30 PM, but stop on in early to warm up! No need to sign up ahead of time, just grab some friends and show up!
